Space Massacre

Space Massacre is a game belonging to the bullet hell genre developed for mobile devices, in the game players will control a small ship armed with a turret, with which we must defeat the numerous hordes of enemies that come to attack us, thus improving our weapons and earning points.

Unity C#
In the game we must withstand as many rounds of enemies as possible.
During the game we will find several powerups that will change our turret or improve it.
The difficulty and number of enemies on the screen will increase during the game.
We will also have to overcome confrontations against bosses
